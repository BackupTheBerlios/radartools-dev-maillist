Hi All,

I am writing a COSAR format reader for stripmap data for now using this
document
http://wwwserv2.go.t-systems-sfr.com/tsx/documentation/TX-GS-DD-3307.pdf
. The format is quite simple for single bursts , but for scansar data
containing multiple bursts reading and displaying in RAT may require
some thought. I will let you know once the code is ready , hopefully
today and would be happy to commit it to SVN and do any maintenance.
Please also let me know you ideas about dealing with the scansar bursts.

Other than that I would also like to add some basic geography support in
RAT to obtain real world coordinates of images, any thoughts in this
direction are also welcome.
