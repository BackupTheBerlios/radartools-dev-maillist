Hello,

from my side, everything seems ok for the release. At least I don't
know of severe problems, which could hamper it.
I don't know the state of the html-doc files, either they have reached
the critical mass for presentation. If yes, one might consider to put
it somewhere on the net, if not, then perhaps later.
I like the idea about a wiki. A combination of idl-doc help-pages with
a public user-driven wiki could be quite powerfull, so that we could
let the users contribute their few cents to the project (e.g. instead
of the post-card [btw. Andreas, do you still get them? and at which
address?], they could be asked for a few lines in the wiki, etc.). But
that's of course something for later versions.

I only briefly looked into the necessary modifications for the real
batch mode. It's possible, and quite useful, but it will involve a lot
of changes to the structure of rat. It's not to be done for this
release, since one will need a lot of testing in addition.

Maxim
